Refreshing Summer Drink

Thirsty? This refreshing summer drink will cool you off!

Have you ever been stuck outside sweating your face off? This refreshing summer drink brings the artic chills right to your front porch! Enjoy with friends, family, or the nextdoor neighbors! Drink responsibly.

Ingredients

  • Selzter Water (Plain or Flavored)
  • Dryck Lingon Lingonberry Syrup
  • Angustura Bitters
  • Crushed Ice

Instructions

  1. In a glass, mix 12 oz selzter water and 1.5 oz lingonberry syrup
  2. Add 2 dashes of Angustura Bitters; stir thoroughly
  3. Top off glass with crushed ice (Bonus: use a frozen glass and garnish with a citrus peel!)
  4. Sip away the blistering sun!
