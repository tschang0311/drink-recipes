Wonderful Water

Is your mouth dry? Try this revolutionary new water!

Wash away your worries with this 5 stage water purification process! Turn you tap water into the springs of Antartica with these easy steps. Never worry about where your water is coming from ever again!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ice
  • Air
  • Crushed Ice
  • Dry Ice

Instructions

  1. In a medium sauce pan, bring 1 oz of ice and 1 L of water to a boil. Make sure to constantly stir to get the best results!
  2. Let mixture cool and pour into a large glass container. Freeze over night.
  3. Take frozen water out of the freezer and place into a fine-mesh strainer. Collect water in a plastic tub and add dry ice. Insert tube and blow air into the bottom of the plastic tub.
  4. After all the dry ice has sublimed, strain once more in a medium to large number colander before marinating in the fridge for a couple of hours.
  5. Serve water in a tall glass with crushed (Tip: Use cubed ice if crushed ice is not available) ice and enjoy knowing that your water is purified down to its molecular makeup!
